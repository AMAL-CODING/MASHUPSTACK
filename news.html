NEWS ABOUT WAYANAD LANDSLIDE

One more body and three more body parts were recovered on Monday as part of the massive search operation held in the aftermath of the devastating landslides at Wayanad. State Revenue Minister K Rajan said the death toll in the July 30 landslides has increased to 231.
